01Why we exist

Why we exist

Dutch logistics is the engine room of European trade — but the warehouses and depots that keep it running operate on a labor market that can't keep pace. Every unfilled shift is a slower line and a missed cut-off. Jeff Logistics closes that gap with a careful, people-first vetting process that puts the right person on the floor fast, cutting downtime and recruitment overhead for operators who can't afford to wait.

"We don't recruit for everyone. We recruit for the floor — and the people who keep it running."

About Us

Six years of placing people first.

We're a new company — but far from new to this work. Our team has spent six years placing workers across Scandinavia before bringing that same approach to the Netherlands.

01Our story

From Scandinavia to the Netherlands.

Jeff Logistics was founded by a team with deep roots in Nordic workforce placement. For six years, we helped hundreds of workers find stable, fairly-paid roles in warehouses, ports, and logistics hubs across Sweden, Norway, and Denmark.

We saw first-hand what happens when workers are treated as people — not headcounts. Productivity goes up. Turnover goes down. Everyone wins.

In 2024, we brought that same philosophy to the Amsterdam region — one of Europe's most important logistics corridors — and we haven't looked back.

02What we believe

Our values

The principles that shape every placement we make.

People over headcount

Every worker we place is a person with goals, not a number to fill a shift. We stay in contact, we check in, and we advocate when things go wrong.

Honest about what we offer

We never over-promise. If a role isn't right for someone, we say so. If a client's expectations aren't realistic, we push back — respectfully.

Compliance is non-negotiable

Every contract, every placement, every invoice — done properly. We're registered, auditable, and Wtta-aligned. No shortcuts.

Speed without cutting corners

Logistics doesn't wait. We move fast — but we never skip the screening, the check-in call, or the paperwork that protects everyone.
